PCB Test Fixture

Mechanical Design

Designed a highly safe and adjustable fixture for PCB testing. The design incorporates a crucial safety interlock to protect the operator from high voltage and a clear acrylic lid for dynamic screw adjustments during testing.

  • **Objective:** Enable safe testing procedures.
  • **Key Features:** Safety interlock, high-voltage protection, clear adjustment visibility.
  • **Technologies:** Designed in **SolidWorks** utilizing **GD&T**, and fabricated using in-house **3D printing**.

Scrap Report - Quality Analysis Tool

Data Analysis & Quality Control

  • **Impact:** Proactive assignment of continuous improvement projects.
  • **Functionality:** Interactive graphs and tables for dynamic filtering.
  • **Technologies:** Created using **PowerBI**. Utilized **SQL** for data extraction and **VBA coding** for automated data transformation.
